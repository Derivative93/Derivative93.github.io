About Me

I am a dedicated law student with a keen interest in sport, technology and economics. I recently reached the quarterfinals of a national Commercial Awareness competition hosted by Aspiring Solicitors. Through this, I gained a broader understanding and knowledge of the business world and the companies that dominate our economy.


I am pursuing a career in commercial law. I am particularly interested in intellectual property, contract law and real estate. I keep myself informed of the latest news and updates surrounding these fields.


In order to maintain my ability to speak, write and understand both French and German, I often practice with foreign friends and read European news articles. I am learning Spanish in my own time and intend to learn both Italian and Polish in the future.


I am a technophile and have built several computers. I follow the latest technology company offerings and I keep abreast of eSports developments. I can type at over 100 words per minute and I am proficient in the use of basic and advanced software.


I regularly attend a pub quiz with friends in my spare time, and also enjoy playing football and tennis. Having completed my grade 7 exam some years ago, I continue to learn new piano pieces to ensure that I retain the skill.
