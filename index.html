About Me

I am a dedicated law student with a keen interest in sport, technology and economics. I recently reached the quarterfinals of a national Commercial Awareness competition hosted by Aspiring Solicitors. I am pursuing a career in commercial law. I am particularly interested in intellectual property, commercial dispute resolution and pensions.


I have highly competent IT skills and have built several computers. I follow the latest technology company offerings and I keep abreast of eSports developments. I can type at over 120 words per minute and I am proficient in the use of basic and advanced software including the Office Suite, LexisNexis and PLC.


In order to maintain my ability to speak, write and understand both French and German, I often practice with foreign friends and read European news articles. I am learning Spanish in my own time and intend to learn both Italian and Polish in the future.


I regularly attend a pub quiz with friends and also enjoy playing football and tennis. Having completed my grade 7 exam some years ago, I continue to learn new piano pieces to ensure that I retain the skill.
